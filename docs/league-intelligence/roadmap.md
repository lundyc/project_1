# Roadmap

## V1: Core Analytics
- League table and standings
- Team profile pages
- Form, streaks, and basic trends
- Head-to-head and home/away splits
- League-wide context panels

## V1.5: Comparison Tools & Reports
- Team-to-team comparison
- League-wide analytics reports
- Export and sharing features

## V2: Advanced Analytics & Visualisation
- Interactive trend visualisations
- Custom league dashboards
- Automated insights and alerts

## Explicitly Not Planned (Yet)
- xG (expected goals) or shot-based analytics
- Player-level analytics or tracking
- Event-level data (shots, passes, pressing)
- Video or image-based analytics
