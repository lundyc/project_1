# Team Profile

The Team Profile page provides a deep, contextual view of a single team's performance within the league.

---

## 1. Team Snapshot
- **Purpose**: Give a high-level summary of the team's current league position and status.
- **Metrics**: Position, Points, Record (W-D-L), Goal Difference, Current Streak.
- **Questions Answered**: Where does this team stand? What is their recent form?

## 2. Form & Momentum
- **Purpose**: Visualize recent results and momentum.
- **Metrics**: Last 5 results, streaks (win/loss/draw), points per game trend.
- **Questions Answered**: Is the team improving, declining, or stable?

## 3. Results & Fixtures
- **Purpose**: Show all past results and upcoming fixtures.
- **Metrics**: List of matches (date, opponent, venue, result), upcoming matches.
- **Questions Answered**: What has the team's journey been? What’s next?

## 4. Performance Profile
- **Purpose**: Summarize key performance indicators.
- **Metrics**: Goals For/Against, Clean Sheets, Average Goals per Match, Home/Away splits.
- **Questions Answered**: How does the team perform overall? Are there strengths or weaknesses?

## 5. Home vs Away Analysis
- **Purpose**: Compare home and away performance.
- **Metrics**: Points per game, win rate, goals for/against (home vs away).
- **Questions Answered**: Is the team stronger at home or away?

## 6. Head-to-Head Intelligence
- **Purpose**: Show performance against specific opponents.
- **Metrics**: Record vs each team, recent head-to-head results.
- **Questions Answered**: Does this team have rivals or “bogey” teams?

## 7. League Context & Difficulty
- **Purpose**: Place the team’s performance in league context.
- **Metrics**: Strength of schedule, average opponent position, upcoming difficulty.
- **Questions Answered**: Has the team had an easy or tough run? What’s coming up?

## 8. Trends & Visual Analytics
- **Purpose**: Surface patterns and trends visually.
- **Metrics**: Points trend, goal difference trend, form streaks.
- **Questions Answered**: What patterns are emerging in the team’s season?
